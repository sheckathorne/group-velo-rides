Each role includes the abilities of the roles below them, even if not explicitly specified.

Creator - The founder of the club. Has complete management control over the club, including the ability to disband it altogether. Once made a creator, the member cannot be removed or demoted to a lesser role.

Admin - Can modify members' roles, respond to membership requests, and remove members.

Ride Leader - Can create events and rides that are associated with the club.

Route Contributor - Can create routes to be used in rides that are associated with the club. Any route created by a Route Contributor are available to all Ride Leaders for use in their rides.

Paid Member - Can join members-only rides that are associated with the club.

Unpaid Member - Can view club rides but cannot join them unles they are set to 'Open'.

Non-Member - Has no club priveleges.
